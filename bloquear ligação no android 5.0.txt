Block a call
==============
Open the Contacts app. contacts
-To block someone, you must first add them to your contacts. Touch the contact that you want to block or add that contact.
-Touch the Edit icon. 
-Touch the Menu icon. Menu
-Tick the box next to "All calls to voicemail".
